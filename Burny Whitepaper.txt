BURNY: The community owned deflationary, reward token on the Binance Smart chain.

Burny is a deflationary, reward token built on the Binance Smart Chain network. It introduces a unique burning mechanism 
where 99% of the token supply has already been burned, and with each transaction, 4% of the transfer is permanently removed 
from circulation. This deflationary design aims to create scarcity and potentially increase the value of Burny over time. 
Also, 1% of transactions goes towards marketing.
